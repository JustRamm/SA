Thanal (The Shade)

Location SPA Bhopal Campus, India
Year 2023
Typology Competition Winner / Built Intervention

Competition to Construction

Thanal was the winning entry of the "Shade Design Competition" at SPA Bhopal. Unlike most competition entries, this project was fully executed on-site — built with steel sections and triangular geometric patterns to create a functional and visually engaging shelter near a popular campus juice shop.

Geometry & Shadow

The triangular steel framework is not purely decorative. Each facet is calculated to maximally diffuse harsh sun while creating a dynamic, ever-shifting pattern of light and shadow on the ground below — transforming a simple sheltered area into a contemplative campus landmark.
