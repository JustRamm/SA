Transforming via Transferring

Location Vyttila, Kochi, Kerala
Year 2024–2025
Typology Transit Hub / Socio-Cultural Nucleus

Concept

Reimagining the Vyttila Mobility Hub as a multifunctional urban landmark. The project explores the potential of transit hubs to act as more than just points of arrival and departure—but as vibrant socio-cultural intersections that integrate three major transport modes: Metro, Water Metro, and Bus, alongside commercial and community spaces.

Spatial Strategy

The design features multi-level zoning to manage complex pedestrian and vehicular flows. A basement accommodates parking, bus terminals occupy the ground and first floors, and a plaza level at the metro concourse serves as a public realm — a living civic space for Kochi's residents.
