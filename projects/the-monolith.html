The Monolith

Location Smart City, Bhopal, India
Year 2024
Typology Hospitality / Five-Star Hotel

Design Philosophy

The Monolith employs strong geometry and dramatic vertical facade elements to create a sense of permanence and prestige. The architectural mass is conceived as a singular, imposing volume — strategically carved to reveal interior functions and allow controlled light to penetrate the guest areas, giving the building life from within.

Operational Strategy

Central to the design is the clear separation between "Front of House" and "Back of House" operations. Guest reception, banquets, and leisure facilities are prioritized for visual prominence, while service corridors and logistics are discreetly integrated for seamless, invisible hotel management.
