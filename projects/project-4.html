Civic Center

Location Chennai, India
Year 2025
Typology Public

Community Nexus

Designed as a gathering space for the modern urban fabric, the Civic Center mediates between the chaotic street level and the serene public library above. A series of ramps and voids carve through the mass, inviting pedestrian flow.

Structure & Light

Exposed waffle slabs create a rhythmic ceiling landscape, while maximizing span for the auditorium below. Light wells penetrate the deep plan, marking key circulation nodes.
